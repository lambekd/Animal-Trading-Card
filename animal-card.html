Bald Eagle

Image courtesy of Moose Henderson/Getty Images

While most birds flee the storm when one arrives, the bald eagle rides the storm in order to gain a higher altitude.

  • Scientific Name: Haliaeetus leucocephalus
  • Average Lifespan: 30-35 years
  • Top Speed: 30 miles per hour
  • Maximum wingspan: 7 feet
  • Habitat: Along the coastline; major lakes and rivers

Since 1782, the bald eagle has been America's national mascot and emblem. The majestic bird of prey lives along the coast lines, preying on fish that swim through the rivers and lakes where they live. Nearly half of the world's bald eagles live in Alaska.
